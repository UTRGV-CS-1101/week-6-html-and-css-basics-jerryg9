Major Characters of the Dance of the Dragons

Key figures from George R.R. Martin's epic Targaryen civil war

Major Characters of the Dance of the Dragons

  • Rhaenyra Targaryen: The firstborn daughter of King Viserys I, she was named his heir and fought for her right to rule. Intelligent, proud, and determined, she was a dragonrider of Syrax.
  • Aegon II Targaryen: Rhaenyra's half-brother, crowned king by the Hightower faction. He rode the dragon Sunfyre and was ruthless in his pursuit of the throne.
  • Daemon Targaryen: Rhaenyra's uncle and husband, a fierce warrior and dragonrider of Caraxes. He was a key commander and strategist for Rhaenyra's cause.
  • Alicent Hightower: Queen to Viserys I and mother to Aegon II. She was a driving force behind her son's claim and a master of court intrigue.
  • Ser Criston Cole: The "Kingmaker," Lord Commander of the Kingsguard. He supported Aegon II and led many battles against Rhaenyra's supporters.
  • Aemond Targaryen: Aegon II's younger brother, known for his cruelty and skill in battle. He rode the mighty dragon Vhagar.
  • Corlys Velaryon: The "Sea Snake," Lord of the Tides. He commanded the Velaryon fleet and was a staunch supporter of Rhaenyra.
  • Baela and Rhaena Targaryen: Daughters of Daemon, both dragonriders and active participants in the war.
  • Lord Larys Strong: The "Clubfoot," master of whispers and intrigue, manipulating events from behind the scenes.
  • Otto Hightower: Alicent's father and Hand of the King, orchestrator of the Greens' political maneuvers.
